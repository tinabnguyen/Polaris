About Us

Polaris is a KPOP cover dance group based in DFW, originally formed in 2018 under a different name (Half and Half). The group started out as a group of friends wanting to learn a dance to a kpop song. The song at the time was Black Suit by Super Junior. Soon the group was requested to perform at a festival and thus officially kicked things off as a kpop cover dance group. The name Polaris was then brought forth in 2021. The group serves as a safe space for members to express themselves freely through their musical interests. The members have a strong bond with one another that was built on mutual respect. Polaris intends to promote camaraderie and teamwork. The team members themselves are passionate and dedicated to every project they work on making Polaris a one of kind team.
